# Tarot-Based Content Generator

## Purpose
To blend spirituality with practical life advice for audience engagement.

## Prompt
Create a short video script explaining how the tarot card [insert card] applies to real-life situations. Include:
- Meaning of the card
- Real-life example
- Advice or lesson

## Example
Input: Queen of Cups

Output:
"The Queen of Cups teaches emotional balance. If you're always pouring into others but feel drained, it's time to protect your energy."
