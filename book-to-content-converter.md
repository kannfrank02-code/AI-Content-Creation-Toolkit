# Book to Content Converter

## Purpose
To transform book chapters into social media content, increasing visibility and sales.

## Prompt
Take the following chapter summary and convert it into:
- 3 TikTok ideas
- 2 Instagram captions
- 1 YouTube topic
- 1 journal prompt

## Example
Input: Chapter about not chasing

Output:
- TikTok: "Why chasing kills attraction"
- Caption: "You are not meant to convince people to love you"
- YouTube: "The psychology behind feminine energy"
- Journal Prompt: "Where am I overextending myself?"
