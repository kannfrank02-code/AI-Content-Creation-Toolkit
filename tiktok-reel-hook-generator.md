# TikTok & Reels Hook Generator

## Purpose
To create attention-grabbing hooks that increase views, engagement, and watch time on short-form content.

## Prompt
Generate 10 viral hooks for TikTok/Reels based on the topic: [insert topic]. Make them emotionally engaging, bold, and relatable.

## Example
Input: Stop proving your worth

Output:
- "Stop explaining your value to people committed to misunderstanding you."
- "You’re not tired—you’re overextending yourself for the wrong people."
- "The moment you stop proving yourself, everything changes."
